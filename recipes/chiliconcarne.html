Description

Chili con carne

Ingredients

  • 500g beens
  • 4 onions
  • 300g minced meat
  • 1 spoon Paprika powder
  • 100g lard
  • 1 red, 1 yellow, 1 green pepper bell
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Add the onions to the preheated lard
  2. Add the meat to it
  3. Add the pepper bells, salt and pepper, cook for 30 mins
